Support Yayasan Islam Darul Ehsan

As a non-profit organization, Rumah Bakti Nur Syaheera is entirely dependent on the kindness and generosity of individuals, corporations, and community groups. Your contribution is vital in allowing us to provide continuous care, education, and a loving home for the children under our wing.

Every donation, no matter the size, makes a tangible difference in the lives of vulnerable children. Thank you for considering supporting our mission.

Where Your Donations Go

We are committed to transparent use of funds. Your contributions primarily support the daily operations and crucial programs for our children:

  • Daily Provisions: Nutritious meals, clean water, and essential daily consumables.
  • Education & Development: School fees, uniforms, textbooks, extra tuition, computer literacy programs, and educational outings.
  • Healthcare & Welfare: Regular medical check-ups, medication, dental care, and emotional counseling.
  • Shelter & Maintenance: Upkeep of our facilities, utilities (electricity, water), and ensuring a safe living environment.
  • Life Skills Programs: Funding for workshops that teach cooking, financial literacy, personal hygiene, and social skills.
  • Operational Costs: Supporting our dedicated caregivers, administrative staff, and overall management of the home.

Other Ways You Can Contribute

  • Volunteer Your Time: Share your skills! We welcome volunteers for tutoring, organizing activities, mentorship, or general home support. See our Contact Us page for more details.
  • In-Kind Donations: We are always grateful for donations of non-perishable food items, new school supplies, children's books, clean used clothing (in good condition), and essential toiletries. Please contact us first to check our current needs.
  • Corporate Sponsorship: Businesses can partner with us through CSR initiatives, sponsoring specific programs or events.
  • Spread Awareness: Help us by sharing our mission and needs with your network!
