Our Story, Our Mission

Discover the heart behind Yayasan Islam Darul Ehsan.

The Beginning of a Haven

Yayasan Islam Darul Ehsan (YIDE) is a charitable institution established by the Selangor Islamic Religious Council (MAIS) to help orphans and needy groups in Selangor. YIDE was established based on MAIS' concern for the welfare of orphans and target groups in Selangor.

From humble beginnings, our home has grown into a beacon of hope, providing comprehensive care that addresses the physical, emotional, and developmental needs of our cherished residents. We are driven by the belief that every life is precious and deserves the opportunity to flourish.

Our Guiding Principles: Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide holistic care, educational support, and a loving, nurturing environment for vulnerable children, empowering them to reach their fullest potential and fostering their independence and dignity.

Our Vision

To be a leading children's home, advocating for child welfare and raising community awareness to build a compassionate society where children are valued, supported, and given every opportunity to succeed.
