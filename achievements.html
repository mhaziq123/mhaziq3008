Our Achievements & Milestones

At Yayasan Islam Darul Ehsan, we celebrate every step forward our children take. These achievements are a testament to their resilience, the dedication of our caregivers, and the unwavering support of our community.

Academic & Educational Success

Excellent UPSR/PT3/SPM Results

We are thrilled to announce that our students consistently achieve commendable results in national examinations, with several scoring distinctions.

Pathways to Higher Education

Several of our former residents have successfully gained admission to public universities and vocational colleges, pursuing their dreams.

Science Fair & Innovation Wins

Our children have participated and won awards in various school and district-level science and innovation competitions, showcasing their bright minds.

Personal Development & Community Contributions

Sports & Extracurricular Excellence

Our children actively participate in school sports and clubs, bringing home medals and recognition in various local competitions.

Creative & Performing Arts

Showcasing their talents in school drama productions, art exhibitions, and traditional dance performances during cultural events.

Active Community Engagement

Our older children regularly volunteer for local charity events and environmental clean-up campaigns, demonstrating their sense of civic responsibility.
